Privacy Policy
Effective date: July 2026 · valuted.in
The short version: We collect nothing about you. We store nothing about your conversations. When your session ends, everything is permanently gone.

What we collect

Nothing that identifies you. Vaulted does not require an account, email address, phone number, or any personal information to use.

The only information that briefly exists on our servers is:

  • The encrypted content of messages in transit (relayed in memory, never written to disk)
  • A temporary session token stored in your browser's localStorage (auto-expires in 5 minutes)
  • Standard server access logs generated by Railway (our hosting provider) which may include IP addresses and request timestamps

How messages work

All messages are end-to-end encrypted using AES-256-GCM with keys generated in your browser. The server never sees your message content — only encrypted ciphertext that it cannot read.

Messages exist only in the server's RAM while being relayed. The moment both parties disconnect or close the room, all message data is permanently deleted. There is no database. There are no backups.

File attachments

Files are encrypted in your browser before upload, transmitted as encrypted data, and delivered directly to the recipient. Files are not stored on our servers.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or any third-party tracking tools. We do not use Google Analytics or any equivalent service.

Third-party services

Vaulted is hosted on Railway (railway.app). Railway may collect standard infrastructure logs including IP addresses. Please review Railway's privacy policy for details. We use Google Fonts for typography — fonts are loaded from Google's servers.

Children's privacy

Vaulted is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
